Pre-Wedding 

Pre-bridal makeup can be defined as makeup for the bride-to-be for functions that are before her wedding day. This can include makeup for her pre-wedding shoot, Mehndi, Sangeet, engagement etc. amongst a dozen other pre-wedding festivities that lead up to a grand wedding.
Firstly, the bride should be happy from inside, so that the natural glow is visible and when the makeup is applied then the face can be more vibrant and beautiful
